BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

 

Business Intelligence helps predict, track, analyze, and present information as it relates to business performance - it provides the tools your company needs to translate data into actionable information.

Gresit can help you with your data governance initiatives by providing the following services:  

  • BI Strategy - Design of BI strategy, data mart and enterprise data warehouse architectures
  • BI Governance  - Proof-of-concept approach for an individual tool selection and BI infrastructure architecture
  • BI Architecture - Governance and review of existing BI infrastructure and BI solutions
  • Operations  of BI Platforms - Operations support and expert desk for BI platforms
  • Maintenance of BI Platforms - Maintenance, optimisation, migration and application management of BI solutions.
